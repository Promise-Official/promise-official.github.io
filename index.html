Calak Perthran had never been a confident individual. The constant verbal harassment from peers causing him to believe his congenital condition to see shadow demons is really only schizophrenia ensured that. He kept his head down through college, yet it wasnâ€™t enough to keep him completely unnoticed. The magic-blessed Queen Eira herself had taken notice of his brilliance and enlisted him as her royal architect. Their friendship blossomed, and they became each other's safe haven. However, a black magic user desired the limitless power of the queen for their own and recklessly attempted to steal it. Unforeseen circumstances turned to what could be the end of the world with only the unlikely duo, along with some trusted allies, to fix it.

Promise is a dystopian fantasy (with a hint of romance) novel authored by Myu Ether as a passion project. This site exists for the purpose of both sharing her love for this project and to encourage others alongside her journey to finish and publish this book. Here, you can find information on the book and its author, the world, the characters, and look forward to the weekly updates on progress. Thank you for being here; you help make this dream project a reality!
